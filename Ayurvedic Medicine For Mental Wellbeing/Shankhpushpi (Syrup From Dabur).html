Dabur Shankhpushpi Syrup is an Ayurvedic formulation that can help improve learning and concentration. It can be beneficial for reducing mental fatigue.

Key Ingredients

1. Shankhpushpi (Convolvulus pluricaulis, PL.)

2. Brahmi (Hydrocotyle asiatica, PL.)

3. Nimbu Satva (Citrus limon, Satva)

4. Sharkara

5. Water

6. Preservatives: Sodium methylparaben I.P., Sodium propylparaben I.P.

Indications

# Improving learning and concentration

# Reducing mental fatigue

Key Benefits

The syrup can help improve learning and concentration

It may aid in minimising mental fatigue

It can improve overall health and well-being
