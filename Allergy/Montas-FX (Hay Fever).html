Montas-FX
 is a combination medication primarily used to treat symptoms of allergic rhinitis (hay fever) and chronic skin allergies like urticaria (hives)
Composition 
Each tablet combines two active ingredients to manage allergy symptoms: 
  • Fexofenadine (120 mg): An antihistamine that reduces itching, sneezing, and watery eyes.
  • Montelukast (10 mg): A leukotriene receptor antagonist that decreases inflammation and swelling in the nasal passages.
Key Uses and Benefits 
  • Allergic Rhinitis: Relieves sneezing, runny nose, and congestion.
  • Urticaria (Hives): Reduces itching, redness, and swelling associated with chronic hives.
  • Nocturnal Symptoms: Helps improve sleep quality by minimizing nighttime congestion.
Important Safety & Usage Information 
  • Dosage: Usually taken as one tablet daily, typically in the evening, with or without food.
  • Restrictions: Avoid consuming with fruit juices (apple, orange, or grapefruit) to ensure effectiveness.
  • Side Effects: Potential side effects include headache, dizziness, and nausea.
  • Interactions: Allow a 2-hour gap if taking antacids containing aluminum or magnesium.
  • Precautions: Consult a doctor if pregnant or breastfeeding.
