Introduction


Otraderm-100 Capsule belongs to a group of medicines called antifungals. It works by stopping the growth of fungi and is used to treat infections of the mouth, throat, vagina, and other parts of the body including fingernails and toenails. It kills fungi by destroying the fungal cell membrane.


Safety advice


Alcohol: CAUTION
Caution is advised when consuming alcohol with Itraconazole Capsule. Please consult your doctor.

Driving: UNSAFE
The Capsule may cause side effects which could affect your ability to drive.
The Capsule can sometimes cause dizziness, blurred/double vision or hearing loss. This may affect your ability to drive.
