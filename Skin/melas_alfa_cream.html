MELAS ALFA 10GM CREAM

Composition:
Hydroquinone 4 %W/W+Mometasone furoate 0.1 %W/W+Tretinoin 0.025 %W/W

What is this medicine for

It is used to lower the color of freckles on the skin.

How does it work

Hydroquinone lightens the skin where there are changes in color.

Mometasone is a steroid (also called a corticosteroid). Steroids help to reduce swelling (inflammation) in the skin (and other parts of the body). Skin gets inflamed when an allergic reaction or irritation causes chemicals to be released in the skin.

Tretinoin, also known as all-trans retinoic acid, is a medication used for the treatment of acne and acute promyelocytic leukemia. For acne, it is applied to the skin as a cream, gel or ointment. For leukemia, it is taken by mouth for up to three months.
