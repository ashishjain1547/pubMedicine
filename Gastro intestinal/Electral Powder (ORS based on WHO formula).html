Information about Electral Powder

    
It is used to restore body fluids and electrolytes lost due to dehydration. It can be used by people suffering from gastrointestinal disorders such as diarrhea and vomiting. It acts as an oral rehydration salt (ORS) formula that has been recommended for use by the World Health Organization (WHO). It is also indicated for the treatment of metabolic acidosis.

It can be used to treat muscle cramping caused due to lack of water and electrolytes in the body. In some cases, people recovering from illnesses or those suffering from heatstroke are also recommended to use this powder. It can also be taken for dehydration caused due to exertion while exercising or due to excessive loss of water in hot and/or humid weather.

In simple terms, ORS is nothing but a mixture of salts and sugar. In diarrhea, your body tends to lose water and electrolytes such as sodium, chloride, potassium, bicarbonate, etc. If the fluid and electrolyte balance in the body is not maintained by replacing the lost salts and water, dehydration occurs. To prevent dehydration, ORS is recommended. The salt and sugar mixture in ORS stimulates water and electrolyte absorption from the gut, thereby aiding in the replacement of the lost salts in case of diarrhea and vomiting.

It is considered to be a safe and most effective treatment for diarrhea (both in kids and adults). It usually doesn't cause any side effects. However, if your condition worsens or if the symptoms still persist, it is best to consult your doctor. 


Key ingredients


Sodium chloride: 2.6 gm
Potassium chloride: 1.5 gm
Sodium citrate: 2.9 gm
Dextrose anhydrous: 13.5 gm
    

Key benefits/uses of Electral Powder

    
Contains sodium chloride which is essential to maintain fluid and electrolyte balance in the body
Helps restore the lost electrolytes and water caused due to dehydration
Helps in the maintenance of water balance and distribution, acid-base balance, heart function, as well as muscle and nerve cell function
Contains potassium which helps maintain kidney and adrenal function
Boosts your energy level, thanks to the presence of dextrose anhydrous, the purest form of dextrose (commonly called glucose)
Helps in the treatment of diarrhea and vomiting by maintaining water-electrolyte balance 
Works by neutralizing excess acid in the blood and urine, thus helping in the treatment of metabolic acidosis
Improves recovery post an illness in adults as well as older people
Is useful to fight and improve symptoms of heatstroke which is quite common during summer
Helps improve fluid levels after excessive loss of water caused post exercising


Directions for use

    
Dissolve the contents of the packet in the amount of drinking water indicated on the packet (which is usually advised to be dissolved in a liter of water).
If drinking water is not available or if you are not sure whether the water is drinkable, boil the water for at least 10 minutes and then let it cool. Add the contents. Stir to mix all the ingredients properly and then you can drink it.


Quick tips for Electral Powder
    
    
Adults can drink the solution directly from a glass, but for kids, use a spoon or a cup to feed. It is advised to not use a bottle to feed a kid. 
Do not mix the contents in milk, soup, fruit juices, etc. Always use water. Also, avoid adding extra sugar or salt to the solution. 
Always make a fresh drink every time and throw away the solution (if there is any unfinished drink). Keeping the drink uncovered or using the drink for more than 24 hours can up the risk of bacterial infections.
Go through the ingredients before using it. If you are allergic to it or any of the components, it is advisable to not use it.
Be careful to use the right amount of water. If you use too little water, the drink could make diarrhea worse.
Do not use tap water as it carries a high risk of contamination which in turn can worsen the condition.
Talk to your doctor if you suffer from any health problems such as kidney disease, diabetes, heart disease, hypertension, or severe and uncontrollable diarrhea/vomiting.
