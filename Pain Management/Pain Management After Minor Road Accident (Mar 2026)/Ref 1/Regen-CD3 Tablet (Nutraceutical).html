Regen-CD3 Tablet is a nutritional supplement prescribed to treat vitamin and mineral deficiencies. It is primarily used to support nerve health, strengthen bones, and improve metabolism. 
Key Uses & Benefits 
Regen-CD3 helps the body perform several vital functions: 
  • Nerve Health: It is commonly used to treat nerve pain and damage associated with conditions like diabetic neuropathy and peripheral neuropathy.
  • Bone Strength: It helps increase calcium stores and aids in the treatment of bone-related disorders like osteoporosis.
  • Energy & Metabolism: It helps the body convert fats and carbohydrates into energy and supports the production of new proteins.
  • Immune & Blood Health: It strengthens the immune system and is essential for forming red blood cells (RBC), which helps prevent anaemia. 
Typical Composition 
While specific formulations can vary slightly by brand, a "CD3" tablet typically contains a combination of: 
  • Vitamins: Methylcobalamin (B12), Vitamin D3, Pyridoxine (B6), and Folic Acid.
  • Minerals: Calcium Carbonate and Chromium Picolinate.
  • Antioxidants/Other: Alpha-Lipoic Acid, Benfotiamine, and Inositol.

Safety Advice 
  • Consult a Doctor: Even though it is a supplement, it contains potent antioxidants and vitamins that may interact with other medications.
  • Usage: It can be taken with or without food, but should ideally be taken at a fixed time daily.
  • Side Effects: Generally safe, but some may experience mild nausea or constipation. 

Tell me about the function of each of the constituent vitamin, mineral and antioxidant.


Regen-CD3 is a multi-ingredient supplement where each component serves a specific biological role, primarily focused on
nerve repair, bone strength, and metabolic regulation. 
1. Vitamins 
These ingredients are essential for cellular repair and the production of red blood cells. 
  • Methylcobalamin (B12): The active form of B12. It is crucial for myelin sheath formation (the protective coating around nerves) and helps in the regeneration of damaged nerve cells.
  • Vitamin D3 (Cholecalciferol): Enhances the absorption of calcium and phosphorus from the intestine, which is vital for maintaining bone density and immune function.
  • Pyridoxine (B6): Involved in the synthesis of neurotransmitters (chemical messengers in the brain) and supports protein and fat metabolism.
  • Folic Acid (B9): Essential for DNA synthesis and the production of healthy red blood cells, helping to prevent certain types of anaemia. 
2. Minerals 
Minerals in this supplement provide the structural building blocks for bones and assist in blood sugar control. 
  • Calcium Carbonate: Directly increases the body's calcium stores to strengthen bones and teeth. It also plays a role in muscle contraction and nerve signalling.
  • Chromium Picolinate: Improves insulin sensitivity, helping the body regulate blood sugar levels more effectively, which is particularly beneficial for those with diabetic neuropathy. 
3. Antioxidants & Specialized Compounds 
These "neuro-protective" ingredients defend cells from damage and improve nerve conduction. 
  • Alpha Lipoic Acid (ALA): A universal antioxidant that neutralises free radicals to protect the brain and nerve tissues from oxidative stress. It also helps regenerate other vitamins like C and E.
  • Benfotiamine: A fat-soluble version of Vitamin B1 (Thiamine). It is more easily absorbed than standard B1 and protects nerves from damage caused by high blood sugar by reducing harmful metabolic by-products.
  • Inositol: Helps improve nerve conduction and cell signalling. It also supports mood regulation and assists in managing insulin resistance.
