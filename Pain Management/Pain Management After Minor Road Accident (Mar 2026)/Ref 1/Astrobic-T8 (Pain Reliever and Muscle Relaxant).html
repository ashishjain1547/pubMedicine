Astrobic-T8
is a combination medication used to treat muscle pain and inflammation and is primarily prescribed for conditions involving both pain and muscle stiffness. 
Composition 
The tablet typically contains two active ingredients: 
  • Etoricoxib (60 mg): A non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that reduces pain and swelling.
  • Thiocolchicoside (8 mg): A muscle relaxant that helps relieve muscle stiffness and spasms.
Key Uses 
  • Musculoskeletal pain: Relief from pain in joints and muscles.
  • Arthritis: Management of symptoms related to osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis.
  • Muscle Spasms: Treatment of stiffness or spasms caused by strains or injuries.
Important Safety Information 
  • Consult a doctor: This is a prescription medication. You must consult a healthcare provider for the correct dosage.
  • Side Effects: Common side effects may include dizziness, stomach pain, nausea, and headache.
  • Precautions: Inform your doctor if you have a history of heart, liver, or kidney disease. It is generally considered unsafe for use during pregnancy.
